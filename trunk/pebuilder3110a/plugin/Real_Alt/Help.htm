PE Builder v3 plugin

Real Alternative Codec

Overview

Real Alternative will allow you to play RealMedia files without having to install Real Player / RealOne Player.

You do need a player that is capable of playing RealMedia. The included Media Player Classic supports it and works very well. Supported: RealAudio (.ra .rpm), RealMedia (.rm .ram .rmvb .rpx .smi .smil), RealText (.rt), ReadPix (.rp), RealMedia embedded in webpages. .smi and .smil files sometimes only play the first part of a clip.

System Requirements

  • BartPE v3.x and XPE (recommended).
  • Your Sound Card Drive.
  • Your VGA Card Drive (recommended).
  • Windows XP SP2 on your host OS.

Instructions

  • 1- Download Real Alternative Codec from Here
  • 2- Setup it on your windows.
  • 3- Download the Plugin from Here
  • 4- Uncompress the Plugin cabient.
  • 5- Run the Get_Files.cmd to collect the needed files.
  • 6- Start your build and enjoy !
